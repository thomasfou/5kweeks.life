5k Weeks

Privacy Policy

Last updated: 23 May 2026

5k Weeks ("we", "us") makes a habit-tracking and life-calendar app. We built it around a simple idea — live intentionally — and we take the same minimalist approach to your data: we collect as little as we can, and we keep what you track on your device.

Plain-language summary: You don't need an account to use 5k Weeks. The habits, goals, and reflections you record live on your device. We don't sell your data, and we don't show you ads.

Information we collect

  • Data you enter in the app — your birth date, life-expectancy horizon, weekly goals, meditation/gratitude/exercise/fasting entries, and notes. This is stored locally on your device. If you enable device backup or sync (e.g. iCloud), a copy may be stored by that service under its own privacy terms.
  • Purchase information — if you buy a Pro upgrade, the transaction is handled by Apple or Google. We receive confirmation of the purchase but not your full payment details.
  • Basic diagnostics — aggregated, non-identifying crash and performance data that help us fix bugs. You can opt out in your device settings.

How we use it

  • To provide the app's features — your life calendar, habit tracking, reminders, and stats.
  • To process and restore Pro purchases.
  • To diagnose crashes and improve reliability and performance.

What we don't do

  • We don't sell or rent your personal information.
  • We don't run third-party advertising.
  • We don't require you to create an account or hand over an email to use the app.

Notifications

5k Weeks can send two reminders a week — one on Monday to set your goals, one on Friday to review them. These are scheduled locally on your device, and you can turn them off anytime in the app or your device settings.

Your choices

You can edit or delete your entries at any time inside the app. Deleting the app removes the data stored on your device. To request anything else, email us at the address below.

Children

5k Weeks is not directed at children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect data from them.

Changes to this policy

If we make material changes, we'll update the date at the top of this page and, where appropriate, note the change in the app.

Contact

Questions about privacy? Email team@5kweeks.life.
